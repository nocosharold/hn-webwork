About Me

My name is Harold, and I’m a Front-end developer based in Pateros, Philippines. With over 2 years of experience in the industry, I’ve had the opportunity to work on a wide range of projects, from simple landing pages to complex web applications. What I love most about web development is the ability to create something from scratch and watch it come to life.

As a developer, my main objective is to enhance user experience and contribute to the betterment of the web. My focus is on developing maintainable, scalable, and efficient applications. I stay up-to-date with the latest technologies by constantly reading, watching, and experimenting with them, to apply modern solutions available in the market.

When I’m not coding, you can find me playing online games, listening to music, or repairing some devices. I enjoy these activities as they help me unwind and recharge my creativity. Additionally, I find that engaging in these hobbies often provides me with new perspectives and ideas that I can bring to my work.
